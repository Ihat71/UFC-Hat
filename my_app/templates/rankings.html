Methodology

The k-factor of our ELO equation is 32, however, it can go up to 36 if the fighter got a knockout, or a submission, and depending on how early the win was. A second round KO shows the difference in levels between two fighters more than a 5 round split decision

However, I only take UFC fights into account.

Keep in mind: ELO rankings in sports like UFC are not always accurate due to the low volume of matches, however they show interesting insights.
